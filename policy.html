Policy

1. Full payment is required to confirm your booking.

2. The number of guests must not exceed the count specified at the time of booking.

3. Only registered guests are permitted to stay at the villa.

4. Valid ID proofs are required from all guests, both during booking and upon arrival.

5. Foreign nationals must provide passport and visa details before their stay.

6. Guests are responsible for any damage caused to the property during their stay, with charges applied accordingly.

7. Illegal activities, including prostitution and narcotics use, are strictly prohibited.

8. Commercial activities are not allowed on the premises.

9. A refundable deposit may be collected before check-in.

10. The deposit is refunded within 5 working days, provided there are no additional guests and check-out occurs on time.

11. For any assistance or grievances, guests can contact info@umbervilla.com
