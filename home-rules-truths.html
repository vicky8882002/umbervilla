Home Rules Truths

1. Please be aware that the path leading to the villa, approximately 1.5 km in length, may not be paved and could be affected by adverse weather conditions.

2. CCTV cameras are strategically positioned to monitor all entrances, ensuring the safety and security of our guests.

3. In the event of a power outage, our 62.5 KVA generator ensures uninterrupted power supply for up to 8 hours. Additional diesel beyond the provided tank will be charged to guests.

4. Rest assured, a caretaker is available on-site to attend to any needs or concerns during your stay.

5. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi connectivity, although network availability may vary.

6. Reliable mobile networks such as Airtel and Vi provide seamless communication within the villa premises.

7. While we encourage laughter and fun, we kindly request guests to keep noise levels to a minimum after 10 PM, respecting the tranquility of the surroundings.

8. Alcohol consumption is permitted, but we ask guests to refrain from carrying food and drinks in and around the pool area for safety reasons.

9. Smoking is only allowed in designated exterior areas of the villa.

10. Only trained dogs are permitted within the villa premises.

11. For hygienic purposes, please wear appropriate swimming attire and shower before entering the pool.

12. We value our villa and staff, and kindly request guests to treat them with the same care and respect as they would their own home and personal staff.

13. Outside vendors for events are not permitted on the property.

14. Delivery services from platforms like Swiggy and Zomato are not available in Alibaug.
