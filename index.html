Living Rooms

The villa boasts two expansive living areas, catering to different needs.

The ground-floor living room accommodates up to 40 guests and features ACs, a portable music system, and Wi-Fi.

On the first floor, a cozy living space awaits with seating for six, equipped with a TV and Wi-Fi connectivity for you to enjoy your favourite movie nights.

Dining Area

On the ground floor you have a formal dining area that offers comfortable seating for up to 12 guests.

Bathrooms

With a total of 8 bathrooms in the villa, including 5 attached, 1 powder, and 2 poolside changing rooms, convenience is assured.

All attached bathrooms feature geysers, towels, essential toiletries, and separate dressing areas.

Indulge in relaxation with bathtubs available in 2 bathrooms.

Kitchen

Guests do not have access to the main or outdoor kitchen facilities.

However, they are provided with a water purifier, crockery, and cutlery.

Pool

Dive into luxury in the expansive private swimming pool, accompanied by an attached gazebo, bar and washrooms.

Measuring 20x40 ft. and 4.5 ft. in depth, the pool provides ample space for enjoyment for all ages.

Pool loungers on the deck allow you to read a book in peace, get a tan while the sun is out or savor your favourite snacks and beverages.

Pool access is available until 11:30 pm.

Lawn

Spread across 10,000 sq. ft., the landscaped lawn features a gazebo and offers seating for up to 40 guests.

Relax in the lounge-worthy seating area, complete with outdoor furniture.

Engage in friendly competition with a game of badminton on the well-maintained lawn or complete your daily walk on the track around it.

Meals

Whether you have a penchant for vegetarian delights or crave the flavours of non-vegetarian cuisine, we've got you covered with our delectable meal options.

  Vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals are offered at an additional cost, ensuring there's something to please every palate.
  For those adhering to Jain dietary restrictions, Jain meals can be arranged upon request.
  Indulge in the quintessential barbecue experience with our barbecue kit/meal option, available at an extra charge.
  While cooking non-vegetarian food is not permitted on-site, guests are welcome to enjoy their favorite non-vegetarian dishes as consumption is allowed.
  To ensure we cater to your culinary preferences seamlessly, kindly inform us of your meal choices in advance, providing a minimum of 48 hours' notice prior to your check-in date.
 

  Guests seeking to explore the local culinary scene can venture to nearby restaurants, bearing in mind the prevailing lockdown regulations. Please verify restaurant timings accordingly.
  Please note that meal prices are exclusive of tax charges, providing clarity on the overall cost.
  We kindly request guests refrain from bringing their own cooks or attempting to prepare meals themselves at the villa.
Access to the kitchen for personal meal preparation is not provided to guests. As delivery services from platforms like Swiggy and Zomato are unavailable in Alibaug, we recommend exploring our in-house dining options or nearby eateries.
Prices and menu items may vary based on availability and peak season rates, ensuring flexibility to accommodate varying preferences and circumstances.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Best Way to Reach:

The property is 15 minutes away from Mandwa Jetty, 105 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, 60 km from Panvel Railway Station, and 56 km from Telipada Bus Stand. Detailed directions will be provided upon booking.

2. Driving to Entrance:

Yes, you can drive directly to the entrance of the villa.

3. Parking Availability:

Parking space is provided for up to 6 cars.

4. Accessibility for Seniors and Wheelchair Users:

Currently, the property is not fully accessible for those with mobility challenges. We're working towards making it more inclusive.

5. Suitable Group Size:

The villa accommodates up to 15 guests when booked as a whole.

6. Society or Standalone:

This is a standalone property.

7. Day Picnics:

The villa can be booked for day picnics at a subsidized cost. Contact us for details.

8. Hosting Events:

Yes, the property is suitable for events. Our team can assist in organizing various events and celebrations.

9. Accommodation for Staff:

Accommodation for 2 staff members can be arranged at an additional cost.

10. Partial Room Bookings:

Yes, you can book select rooms. Contact us for details.

11. Pet Policy:

Trained dogs are allowed, with a maximum of 2 pets at an additional charge.

12. Shoot Reservations:

The property is available for shoots at a separate cost.

13. Staff Availability:

A caretaker is present on-site to assist guests, and housekeeping is provided.

14. Meal Options:

Vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals can be provided at an extra cost. Outside cooking is not allowed.

15. Kitchen Facilities:

The main kitchen is equipped with essential appliances and utensils.

16. Turndown Service:

Linen is changed upon request to conserve water.

17. Wi-Fi and Power Backup:

Wi-Fi is available, and a generator ensures power backup during outages.

18. Additional Amenities:

Barbecue kits and meals can be arranged for an extra charge.

19. Activities at the Villa:

Guests can enjoy swimming, games, barbecue, and relaxation indoors and outdoors.

20. Local Activities:

21. ID Requirements:

Guests may be asked to provide valid IDs upon check-in.

22. Alcohol and Smoking:

27. Restricted Areas:

Additional guests may be entertained with prior permission and within reasonable limits.
